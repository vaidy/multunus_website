We spoke at Droidcon, Bangalore that happened on Nov 18th and 19th. We had two interesting talks on both days.





### [1. Continuous Delivery for an Android application](http://funnel.hasgeek.com/droidcon/83-continuous-delivery-for-an-android-application)


![](/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/vaidy-leena.png)This talk was on First day of the Droidcon conference given by [Vaidy](http://www.multunus.com/work/team/vaidy) and [Leena](http://www.multunus.com/work/team/leena). In this talk, we spoke on what the first step towards moving to a CD setup is. Specifically demonstrated setting up a Push Button deployment system using Ant and Jenkins. [More details here](http://funnel.hasgeek.com/droidcon/83-continuous-delivery-for-an-android-application). 









### [2. Introduction to OpenGL in Android](http://funnel.hasgeek.com/droidcon/94-introduction-to-opengl-in-android)


![](/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/tamil-and-kp.png)This talk was on Second day of the Droidcon given by [Tamil ](http://www.multunus.com/work/team/tamil)and [KP](http://www.multunus.com/work/team/krishnaprasad). The objective of talk was to have people get started with OpenGL programming which can seem to be a little heavy but it need not be. The talk started with a brief introduction to OpenGL and then had quick step-by-step tutorial demonstration. [More details here](http://funnel.hasgeek.com/droidcon/94-introduction-to-opengl-in-android).
