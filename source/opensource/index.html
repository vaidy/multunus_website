Open Source

Without Open Source software, our business would perhaps have been non-existent. We rely on the hard work and generosity of many many individuals and companies - who have made their software open source.

We've just started on our own journey of giving back to the community. But please do come back and visit this page every couple of months. You should see some new stuff here.

Here's what we've been up to so far:

Team Timeline

We've been struggling to hire good talent for a while now. While this is a problem almost every company out there is also facing, its even harder for a young company - who've yet to establish their brand. Showcasing our on-board talent is a nice way to attract people who would otherwise have shied away - because of lack of sufficient insight into the company.

So, we created the timeline:

  • See the demo
  • Checkout the Github repo

If you're facing a hiring challenge in your company, try showcasing your existing talent using this timeline tool. And if it works for you too, let us know at info@multunus.com. We'd love to see your timeline and hear your story :).

Jekyll Author Plugin

This website is built using Jekyll - a static site generator that is blog aware. We used to be on Wordpress - but that’s another story.

If you’re new to Jekyll, but are into Ruby and are looking for a solution for building a flexible, maintainable and fast site - do checkout Jekyll.

But if you’re already a Jekyll fan, you know that there’s a whole bunch of awesome plugins out there to help you build your site. The one thing that we could however not find was something to filter our blog posts by Author.

And that is exactly what this project is about. Simple enough - but useful. :)
